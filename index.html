ABOUT LE BAND

LE BAND is the next phase in African live music. The three man group which won the Best New Artist & Best group Video of the Year Awards in the 2017 Pulse Music Video Awards already packs a repertoire of songs outlining the dynamic nature of generation finding its voice and striking all the right cords while at it.Fidel, Ken and Joel are shaping the future of music by tapping into its rich past and employing powerful, catchy lyrics and their signature rhythms indicating a continent on the verge of global explosion. With all three having turned away from careers considered proper in their motherland Kenya, this rebellion and unwavering commitment to the craft and beauty of music shows in the sort of work they have gone about creating.
